WELCOME TO PINE CITY MALL

An impressive 48 632m² The Pine City Mall is
located on Old Pretoria Road in the
fast-growing, Pine City central business district
and is the only mall of its kind in the region.
The centre was built around massive, awe-inspiring
granite boulders, which are a great conversation
piece and perfect meeting point for shoppers.
A welcome and trusted shopping destination
since 2022, Pine City offers a convenient mix of
major retailers like Pick n Pay and Game, fashion
stores like Truworths and Mr Price, a health gym
and several fast food outlets. The mall also has
specialist offices housing health services such as
dentists. Pine City is positioned as a safe,
customer-friendly mall with 24-hour on-site security
and offers a relaxed, interesting and pleasant
shopping experience.
